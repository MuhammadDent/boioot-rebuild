We need to correct the customer role classification again.

IMPORTANT BUSINESS CLARIFICATION

The customer group must include real-estate brokers as well.
Previously, brokers were omitted or confused with agents.

CORRECT CUSTOMER ROLE MODEL

1) Direct customer roles (primary monetizable accounts)
These are direct platform customers who can subscribe themselves:
- User
- Owner
- Broker
- Office
- CompanyOwner / DevelopmentCompany

2) Child / subordinate roles
These are linked accounts created under higher business accounts:
- Agent

IMPORTANT DIFFERENCE
- Broker is a direct customer account with permissions similar to Office but with lower limits
- Broker CANNOT create agents
- Agent is a subordinate account created by Offices or Companies
- Agent is not equivalent to Broker

GOAL
Update the admin/customer classification so brokers are clearly included in customers,
while agents are treated as subordinate business members.

- User
